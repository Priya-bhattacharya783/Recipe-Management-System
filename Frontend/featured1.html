Strawberry Custard Recipe


Prep Time: 10 minutes

Cook Time: 15 minutes

Total Time: 25 minutes

Servings: 4 servings

Ingredients

  • Milk - 2 cups
  • Sugar - Â½ cup
  • Strawberry Custard Powder or Vanilla Custard Powder - 2 to 3 tbsp
  • Strawberries - 1 cup chopped finely

Instructions

  1. Take custard powder in a bowl and add little milk and make it into a pouring like consistency.
  2. Take milk in a sauce pan, add sugar and bring it to a boil.
  3. Now reduce flame and add in custard mix and keep cooking till it thickens.
  4. Remove from heat and add chopped strawberries.
  5. Now pour it into serving bowls, top with more strawberry and chill in fridge for 3 hours.
  6. Serve cold.
