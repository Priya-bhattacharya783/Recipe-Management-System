Egg Drop Curry Recipe


Prep Time: 10 minutes

Cook Time: 15 minutes

Total Time: 25 minutes

Servings: 4 servings

Ingredients

  • Eggs - 4
  • Onions - 2 medium (finely chopped)
  • Tomatoes - 2 medium (chopped)
  • Green Chilies - 2 (slit)
  • Ginger-Garlic Paste - 1 tbsp
  • Turmeric Powder - ½ tsp
  • Red Chili Powder - 1 tsp
  • Coriander Powder - 1 tsp
  • Garam Masala - ½ tsp
  • Fresh Coriander Leaves - 2 tbsp (chopped)
  • Oil - 2 tbsp
  • Salt - as required
  • Water - 2 cups

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a pan, add chopped onions and sauté until golden brown.
  2. Add ginger-garlic paste and green chilies. Fry until the raw smell disappears.
  3. Add chopped tomatoes and cook until soft and mushy.
  4. Mix in turmeric, red chili powder, coriander powder, and salt. Cook for 2–3 minutes.
  5. Pour in 2 cups of water and bring the curry to a boil.
  6. Reduce the flame, slowly pour in beaten eggs while stirring gently so that thin egg strands form.
  7. Let it simmer for 4–5 minutes. Add garam masala and chopped coriander leaves.
  8. Serve hot with steamed rice or roti.
