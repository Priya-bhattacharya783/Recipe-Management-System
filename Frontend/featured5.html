Masala Fish Fry Recipe


Prep Time: 15 minutes

Cook Time: 15 minutes

Total Time: 30 minutes

Servings: 4 servings

Ingredients

  • Turmeric Powder - ¼ tsp
  • Coriander Powder - 1 tsp
  • Cumin Powder - ½ tsp
  • Garam Masala - ½ tsp
  • Lemon Juice - 1 tbsp
  • Rice Flour / Gram Flour - 2 tbsp (for coating)
  • Salt - as required
  • Oil - for shallow frying
  • Fresh Coriander Leaves & Lemon Wedges - for garnish

Instructions

  1. Clean and wash the fish fillets well. Pat dry with a kitchen towel.
  2. In a bowl, mix ginger-garlic paste, red chili powder, turmeric, coriander powder, cumin powder, garam masala, lemon juice, and salt to form a thick marinade.
  3. Coat the fish pieces evenly with the marinade and let them rest for 30 minutes (or longer for deeper flavor).
  4. Sprinkle rice flour or gram flour lightly over the marinated fish for a crisp outer layer.
  5. Heat oil in a pan on medium flame and shallow fry the fish pieces until golden brown and cooked through on both sides.
  6. Remove and drain on paper towels.
  7. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and lemon wedges. Serve hot with onions or chutney.
